Agent Authority Declaration

This declaration applies when a real estate agent signs the SiteScop Client Inspection Agreement on behalf of the purchaser/client named in the agreement.

Important: Only sign if you are the Agent named in the agreement and you have the Client's express authority to accept this agreement on their behalf.

1. Identity

The Agent confirms they are the authorised real estate agent (or authorised representative of the listed agency) named in the Inspection Agreement.

2. Authority to act

The Agent confirms they have the express authority of the Client to accept this SiteScop Inspection Agreement on the Client's behalf for the nominated property.

3. Client awareness

The Agent confirms they have explained to the Client (or will promptly provide the Client with) the Scope of Inspection, Inspection Limitations, Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy forming part of this agreement; and that the Inspection Report is prepared for the Client only.

4. Binding effect

The Agent understands their electronic signature has the same legal effect as a handwritten signature to the extent permitted by Australian law, and binds the Client to this agreement as the Client's authorised representative.

5. Agent responsibility

SiteScop Pty Ltd relies on this declaration. The Agent accepts responsibility for ensuring they are authorised to sign for the Client. The Agent must not sign unless they hold that authority.

The Client remains the party to whom the inspection is provided. This agreement does not permit third parties to rely on the Inspection Report without SiteScop's written consent.
