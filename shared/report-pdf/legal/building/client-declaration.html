Client Declaration

This declaration forms part of the SiteScop Client Inspection Agreement and must be read together with the Scope of Inspection, Inspection Limitations, Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy.

1. Client Acknowledgement

By accepting this Agreement and providing an electronic signature, the Client acknowledges that they have read, understood and accepted all documents forming part of the Inspection Agreement.

  • I have read the Scope of Inspection.
  • I have read the Inspection Limitations.
  • I have read the Terms & Conditions.
  • I have read the Privacy Policy.
  • I understand the inspection is visual only.
  • I understand inaccessible areas cannot be inspected.
  • I understand concealed defects may exist.
  • I understand recommendations should be followed where applicable.
The Client confirms that sufficient opportunity has been provided to review all Inspection Agreement documents before accepting this Agreement.

2. Inspection Authority

The Client authorises SiteScop Pty Ltd to carry out the requested Building Inspection at the nominated property in accordance with the agreed scope of inspection.

3. Electronic Signature

The Client acknowledges that an electronic signature, including a signature created using a mouse, finger or stylus, constitutes the Client's acceptance of this Inspection Agreement. The Client agrees that the electronic signature has the same legal effect as a handwritten signature to the extent permitted by applicable Australian law.

4. Accuracy of Information

The Client confirms that the information provided to SiteScop Pty Ltd, including contact details and property details, is true and correct to the best of their knowledge. The Client agrees to promptly notify SiteScop Pty Ltd if any material information changes before the inspection is carried out.

5. Inspection Report

The Client understands that the Inspection Report is prepared solely for the Client named within this Agreement and only for the purpose for which the inspection was commissioned. The Inspection Report must be read in its entirety together with all photographs, recommendations and supporting documentation. The Client understands that recommendations for further investigation should be completed by appropriately qualified specialists before relying upon the condition of the property.

6. Privacy Consent

The Client consents to SiteScop Pty Ltd collecting, storing and using personal information for the purposes of booking, conducting and administering the inspection, preparing the Inspection Report and communicating with the Client regarding the inspection. Personal information will be handled in accordance with the SiteScop Privacy Policy.

7. Acceptance of Agreement

By signing this Agreement, the Client confirms that they have:

  • Read the complete Inspection Agreement.
  • Understood the Scope of Inspection.
  • Accepted the Inspection Limitations.
  • Accepted the Terms and Conditions.
  • Read the Privacy Policy.
  • Authorised SiteScop Pty Ltd to carry out the inspection.
  • Provided informed consent before the inspection commences.
The Client should not sign this Agreement unless they have had sufficient opportunity to read and understand all Inspection Agreement documents. If the Client does not understand any part of this Agreement, they should seek clarification before signing.

End of Client Declaration
